A native desktop audio visualizer
built with Electron and Angular 2

Download for Windows & macOS

SKQW (pronounced "skew") allows you to visualize the audio output of you computer's sound card. It is inspired by my love of the old Winamp plugins like Milkdrop and Geiss. Visualizations are written in JavaScript and are rendered with HTML Canvas.
